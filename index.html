The Legacy of Mihaly Huszka (1933–2022)

Huszka Weightlifting is not just a gym; it is the continuation of a lifetime dedicated to iron. Our foundation was built by Mihaly Huszka, a two-time Olympian and world-renowned Hungarian weightlifter.

Mihaly didn't just lift; he taught. He brought the secrets of the Hungarian system—focusing on impeccable technique, explosive power, and longevity—to Texas. Today, we honor his memory by training with the same discipline, precision, and heart that took him to the world stage.

When you train here, you aren't just moving weight. You are stepping into a lineage of excellence.
