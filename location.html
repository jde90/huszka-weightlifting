Parking & Finding Us

1

Park at Rock Chiropractic

When you arrive, park in the Rock Chiropractic parking lot. This is your main landmark.

2

Look for the Warehouse

Huszka Weightlifting is located in the warehouse building behind Rock Chiropractic. Walk through the parking lot to reach the gym entrance.

⚠️

Important Navigation Note

Google Maps may direct you to a parking lot next to the gym.

If this happens:

  • Exit that parking lot
  • Turn right onto the main road
  • Then turn into the entrance for Rock Chiropractic
  • Park there and walk to the warehouse behind

Having trouble finding us? Give us a call at (512) 577-0122 and we'll guide you in.
