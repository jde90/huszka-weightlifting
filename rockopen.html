Meet Us On The Platform

Get ready for the 2026 edition of ROCKTOBER – The Rock Open, presented by Huszka Weightlifting Club.

All ages, bodyweight categories, and experience levels are welcome. Whether this is your first meet or your next step toward a bigger total, come compete with Central Texas's weightlifting community.

This is a USA Weightlifting sanctioned event. All competitors must hold a current USA Weightlifting membership in good standing. Singlets are required for both weigh-ins and competition.
