Off Campus P.E. (OCPE)

Approved OCPE Provider

Huszka Weightlifting Club is an approved Off Campus P.E. (OCPE) provider for both Leander ISD and Round Rock ISD. OCPE is an option in a school district to award high school credits and/or middle school credits for physical education for appropriate private or commercially-sponsored physical activity agencies as an alternative to in-school P.E.

Agencies must meet exceptional quality standards to be considered for approval.

🏆

Level 1

National Competition Level

  • ✓ 15 hours per week of training
  • ✓ Competition at National level
  • ✓ Typically grants an off-period

Note: Huszka Weightlifting Club trains multiple Nationally competitive youth athletes.

💪

Level 2

Alternative Participation

  • ✓ 5 hours per week of training
  • ✓ Earn P.E. credits
  • ✓ Does not provide off-period
